Being and Time - Martin Heidegger (1927)
I read this across 2022, following along Hubert Dreyfus' lectures and making these quite detailed notes. It was excruciating and rewarding in equal parts, and I ended up stopping after chapter II of division two. Maybe I'll finish it one day...
