Expository Articles & Other Publications
Incompleteness Poster
A poster made by myself and my best friend Satbhav Voleti (you can find him here ) on Gödel’s incompleteness theorems, which we presented in the annual department day at our college. This was in 2022.
The Concept of Size
An expository article I wrote in the summer of 2022, comparing a few intertwining notions of size used often in mathematics. So far unpublished.
Incompleteness Theorems
An expository article I co-authored with Satbhav on Gödel’s incompleteness theorems. It was published in 2021 in the Indian Science Cruiser journal. (As a matter of fact, this was my first publication in an academic journal.)
The Promised Strand
An article I wrote for Cogito-137, a student-run science-communication magazine about vaccines in the context of the coronavirus pandemic. It was published in 2020. The version here is missing the references; you may find the complete one here, on the website for Cogito.
Bell's Theorem
An expository article I wrote in 2020 on Bell's theorem. It was supposed to be published in another student-run science communication magazine called Inventa, but I'm unsure if it ever happened. In any case, here's the essay (and do note that all references are missing in this version!).
The Art of Teaching
Another article I wrote for Cogito-137 on Teacher's Day in 2020, in which I attempted articulate the manners in which I saw the pandemic perturb the act of teaching.
Pandemics as Paradigm Shifts
A short essay I wrote in 2020 as part of my first-year biology coursework. It didn't fetch me many marks, but I'm fond of it.
