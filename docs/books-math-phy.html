Notes I've made on various scientific and mathematical texts I read over the years independent of coursework.
Commutative Algebra (Atiyah)
Read around half the text over the summer of 2022 after my third year.
Quantum Mechanics for Thinkers (Auletta & Wang)
An excellent, comprehensive textbook on quantum mechanics I read and took notes on in my second year, 2021.
The Continuum (Weyl)
2021. Read it as a supplementary to my first real analysis course.
Symmetry (Weyl)
2020. Was a tougher read than expected.
The Direction of Time (Reichenbach)
One of my favourites! 2020.
Society of Mind (Minsky)
A lovely book explaining various ideas in cognitive science, written by Marvin Minsky. I made no notes, but simply jotted down some of my favourite quotes from it. 2019.
